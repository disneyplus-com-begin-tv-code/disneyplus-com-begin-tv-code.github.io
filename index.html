What is 8 Digit Disney Plus Code?

The 8-digit Disney Plus code is a unique combination of eight characters that you receive when you log in to your Disney+ account on a new device. This code is used to link your Disney+ account with the specific device, ensuring that you have authorized access to stream content. You'll need to enter this code on Disneyplus.com/begin to complete the activation process.


How do I SignUp for DisneyPlus?

  1. Open your browser and go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  2. Click on the “Sign Up Now” button on the homepage.
  3. Provide a valid email address that you want to associate with your Disney+ account. Click "Continue."
  4. Choose a strong password for your account. Make sure it’s something you can remember but hard for others to guess.
  5. Select the subscription plan that suits you. Disney+ offers different plans, including monthly and annual subscriptions.
  6. Enter your payment details. Disney+ accepts various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and PayPal.
  7. Once your payment is processed, your account is set up. You can now log in and start streaming your favorite Disney+ content.

How do I activate Disneyplus.com/begin account?

  1. Open the Disney+ app on your smart TV, streaming device, or gaming console.
  2. Select the "Log In" option. An 8-digit activation code will appear on your screen.
  3. On a computer or mobile browser, go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  4. Enter the 8-digit code shown on your TV screen into the field on the Disney plus begin page.
  5. Use your email and password to log in to your Disney+ account.
  6. Follow any additional prompts to complete the activation. Once done, your device will be linked to your Disney+ account and ready for streaming.

Alternative Activation Methods:

  • Mobile App: Open Disney+ app, go to Account > Activate Device.
  • Smart TV: Look for Disney+ app, select "Sign In" or "Activate Account."
